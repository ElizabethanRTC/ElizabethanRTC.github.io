What's the Weather?

Project by E. Labno

The closer to the equator we get the higher the temperature. Now what about clouds and the wind? The purpose of this project is re-establish to relationship between latitude and temperature using data from 500 random cities throughout the world and also investigate if there are additional relationships between humidity, cloudiness, wind speed and latitude.
